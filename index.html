Drawing Club

Abduzhapparova Leylo

My name is Leila. I believe that hobbies make our life more colorful. My favorite hobby since childhood is drawing. So when I turned 17, I opened a drawing club. And to this day, throughout the year, I teach talented students of our university and talk about the details of drawing through the prism of painting.
What do we do at the club?
On class days, we review artists and their paintings. I also teach my students to draw with pencils and different colors.
On weekends, we visit art galleries or art exhibitions in our city. We also often go on excursions to the fields, the river or the forest to get inspired.
